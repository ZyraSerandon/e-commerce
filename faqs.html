Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


What is Ate Luz's Halo-Halo?

Ate Luz's Halo-Halo is a popular e-commerce website that specializes in selling authentic and delicious Filipino Halo-Halo, a traditional dessert made with shaved ice, sweetened beans, fruits, and topped with ice cream and leche flan.

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our product selection on the website, add your desired items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Please choose the most convenient payment option for you during the checkout process.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services to various locations. Delivery charges and times may vary depending on your location. You can find more information about our delivery options at checkout.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your delivery on our website or through the courier's tracking system.

What is your return and refund policy?

We strive to provide the best quality products, but if you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 14 days of receipt for a refund or exchange. Please refer to our Return and Refund Policy for detailed instructions.

How can I contact customer service?

If you have any questions or need assistance, you can reach our customer service team by emailing zesrhel@gmail.com or calling +012 345 67890. Our team is available to help you with any inquiries you may have.
